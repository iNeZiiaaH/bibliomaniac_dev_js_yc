Alina Starkov’s story closely follows the structure of the classic hero’s journey

Leigh Barudo

Today . 7 min read

Alina’s journey is chronicling her transformation from a common orphan to a symbol of hope for the people of her homeland. Her own journey begins when she learns that she is the Sun Summoner after entering the unknown reaches of the Shadow Fold and unconsciously triggering a long-dormant power that she possesses when her oldest friend is attacked by the volcra.
